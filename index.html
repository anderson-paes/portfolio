UX/UI for a payment App

Zapay's challenge was to create the initial mobile version of their web payment service for vehicles licenses and taxes. A highlight of this work is the reachability feature applied to the multiple selection list. The solution for this feature, in addition to accessibility, contributes for the decision-making and not overload personal memory, making it easier to navigate and review what was previously selected.
